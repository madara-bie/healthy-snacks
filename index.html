Energy bites

What will You need and what to do

  • Pistachios
  • Coconut flakes
  • Peanut butter
  • Linen seeds
  • Maple syrup
  • Dried cranberries

To make this masterpiece, You will need less than 10min. So let`s start!
Blend together all the ingredients - pistachios, coconut flakes, linen seeds and dried cranberries. Mix together with peanutbutter and maple syrup. Actually if You prefare the bites less sweet, You can skip the maple syrup. I am using it because it helps to keep everything together. Probably You are wondering how much of everything You should use? Do how You like the best - there is no right or wrong, because either way it will taste amazing. If You prefare more salty taste, take more of salty pistachios and less syrup. If You want more sour taste, use more cranberries. If You want the bites to be extra sweet, use more of maple syrup or any other syrup by Your choice. When everything is mixed together then take a plate, make rolls by Your hands and put them to rest in a fridge. It is enough to rest them for 15min.
